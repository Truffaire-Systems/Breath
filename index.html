The Farmer Journey

From your field
to your bank account

Seven simple steps. Works on any Android phone. Fully offline in Hassan and Chikmagalur.

📱
01
Registration

Download Breath.
Register in 5 minutes.

Open the app, enter your mobile number, and verify with a one-time OTP. No passwords. No email. No paperwork. Your Aadhaar is verified securely on your device — it never leaves your phone.

🔒 Aadhaar verified on-device
📶 Works offline
🇮🇳 Kannada + English
⚡ Under 5 minutes
🏦
02
Bank Linking

Link your bank.
Get paid directly.

Enter your bank account number and IFSC code. Breath verifies your account with a penny-drop confirmation — a small test deposit confirms everything is correct before your first payment is sent.

🏦 Any Indian bank
✅ Penny-drop verification
🔐 AES-256 encrypted
💸 NEFT or IMPS
🛰️
03
Farm Registration

Stand on your land.
Satellite finds the boundary.

You don't need to walk your boundary. Just stand anywhere on your field and tap "Detect My Farm." Breath uses Sentinel-2 satellite imagery to automatically detect your parcel boundary from space. Confirm it looks right. Done.

🛰️ Sentinel-2 auto-detection
📍 One tap registration
🗺️ Offline map tiles
📐 Area calculated instantly
📸
04
Photo Evidence

One photo.
AI does the rest.

Take one photo from the edge of your field. Breath's AI model compares your photo against what the satellite sees at that exact location. If they match — your farm is confirmed. The GPS coordinates and timestamp are automatically embedded in every photo.

🤖 AI photo verification
📌 GPS + timestamp embedded
☁️ Queued offline, syncs later
🔍 Cross-checked vs satellite
🌱
05
Practice Logging

Log what you grow.
Earn what you change.

Every season, log your farming practices in Breath — what crop you planted, how you treated the soil, what fertiliser you used, whether you left or burned your residue. These practice changes are what generate carbon credits. The more you improve, the more you earn.

🌾 Cover crops
🚜 Tillage type
💧 Irrigation method
🌿 Residue management
🎙️ Voice input supported
✅
06
Satellite Verification

Satellite watches.
Every 5 days. Always.

Sentinel-2 passes over Hassan and Chikmagalur every 5 days and observes every enrolled parcel. It checks that what you logged matches what it sees from space — the crop, the soil condition, the land use. No agent visits. No manual checks. The satellite never lies.

🛰️ 5-day revisit cycle
🌧️ SAR for monsoon season
🌿 NDVI crop health
🔗 Tamper-proof audit trail
💰
07
Payment

Credits issued.
Money in your account.

Once your practices are satellite-verified and your credits are calculated, a corporate buyer purchases them to offset their emissions. The payment flows directly to your registered bank account within 24 hours. You get a push notification the moment it lands — in Kannada.

⚡ Within 24 hours
📲 Push notification
🏦 Direct to bank account
📄 Full payment history
The Verification Layer

Space watches
over your fields

No field agents. No trust issues. Sentinel-2 satellite observes every enrolled parcel every 5 days. The satellite is your independent verifier — it never fabricates data.

🛰️
Sentinel-2 · 10m resolution · 5-day cycle
European Space Agency satellite captures NDVI, soil moisture, and crop classification for every parcel every 5 days. During Karnataka monsoon, SAR radar imagery penetrates cloud cover.
🤖
Karnataka-specific AI models
Crop classification and soil organic carbon proxy models trained specifically on Karnataka red and black soil farms. More accurate than global models.
🔗
SHA-256 cryptographic audit trail
Every data point — from your first photo to your final credit — is hashed and stored in an append-only audit log. Tamper-proof. Registry-defensible.
